tại sao nội dung viết trên web cần quan tâm với người đọc hơn cả những nên tảng khác 

Uncomfortable: They’re reading on a bright screen, holding a phone or staring at their laptop. • Always adjusting: Because there is no standard width or font size, content layout has difficulty staying consistent. • Goal-oriented: There is very little casual reading on the web, especially when you’re selling a product or a solution. • Diverse: You can rarely count on a single, universal audience to write for; instead, you are tasked with writing for everyone. • Impatient: For all of the reasons above, they don’t want to dawdle around.
