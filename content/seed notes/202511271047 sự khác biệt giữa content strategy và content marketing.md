Content strategy – literally the process of making strategic choices about your content – is a high-level process. It’s an important look at multiple angles: what do users want, what do they expect, how can we help, and how can we influence them to choose our service or organization. Content marketing is one possible tactic within this process, and it is exactly what it sounds like: using content to market your services. Content marketing is focused on providing longer and larger-form content, specifically marketed as the product itself, usually with an added benefit of increased lead generation
