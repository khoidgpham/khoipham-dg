Remember, documentation is a communication tool. It’s designed for people who aren’t in the room. Strategic thinking is ethereal, and the decisions that are made within a conference room only exist as far as they are broadcast. If they’re not written down, they are lost. Strategic documentation is a representation of the actual work, not the actual work itself. It is a tool for approval and buy-in. It drives change, but it is not change itself.
