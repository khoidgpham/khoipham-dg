You might notice in this example that instead of the more familiar RACI (responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed) matrix (which I find to be a bit confusing), I’ve replaced the letters with OCUA, something I made up because it made more sense to me. Here’s what those mean: 
1. Owns—The person responsible for making the activity and associated artifacts/deliverables happen 
2. Collaborates—The person or people who work with the owner or provide inputs into the artifacts/deliverables 
3. Uses—The person or people who rely on the artifacts/deliverables to do their work
4. Approves—The person who determines if the artifacts/deliverables are acceptable and done
