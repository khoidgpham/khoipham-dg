# Làm sao để thực hiện Candidate-market-fit statement
Các bước để xây dựng tuyên bộ candiate market fit
1. Pair up with a member of your JSC who has also completed their Listening Tour 
2. Present ndings — especially whether your goals and Candidate-Market Fit are in alignment Present your Mnookin Two-Pager, your key career goal, your notes/journal, and what you learned about your Candidate-Market Fit. Include any notes on the disconnect between what you may want (your career goal) and what the market sees for you today. 
3. Brainstorm a one-sentence statement Run a simple brainstorming session. If you aren’t familiar with some simple brainstorming techniques, then the rm IDEO has some great brainstorming resources online here: https://www.ideou.com/pages/brainstorming (or just search for “IDEO brainstorming”). If you are meeting with your JSC partner via Zoom, then I also recommend you consider using a tool like Mural, which can make brainstorming easy.
4. Outline a multi-step strategy if you can’t get to your goal right away If your goal is out of reach right away, then gure out a multi-step strategy to get there the way that Andy did in the story above.
5. Switch Now do the same three steps for the other member. 
6. Bring your potential statements to your next JSC to present If your strategy is not as obvious as in some of the examples I’ve shown, then perhaps plan on holding a few sessions with your JSC partner.
