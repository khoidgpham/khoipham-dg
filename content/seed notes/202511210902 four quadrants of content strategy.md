> Surrounding the content purpose are four quadrants: 
> 1. Substance defines what content the organization should produce, how it should sound, and why it’s meaningful or relevant to users. 
> 2. Structure refers to how content is organized and displayed so users can find and use the content they need. 
> 3. Workflow is how content flows through the organization—from ideation to distribution to ongoing maintenance.
