The pyramid in the image conveys five stages of information needs that people must have to make their decision and what kind of content my client could provide to satisfy those needs. 
1. Absorb: At this stage, people are just getting a sense for the foundational information they need to understand a product, service, situation, and so on. 
2. Align: At this stage, they are parsing information to determine what aspects match their values and priorities. 
3. Apply: Here, they are imagining how what they’ve learned fits in with their daily lives or presumed needs. 
4. Assess: At this stage, they are digging deeper into what makes sense for them and likely comparing options. 
5. Act: Now, they have decided on a course of action!
