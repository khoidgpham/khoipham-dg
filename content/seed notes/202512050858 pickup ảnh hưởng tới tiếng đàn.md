# Các yếu tố liên quan về pick-up ảnh tới tiếng đàn
- **Vị trí**: Imagine the body of the bass like an EQ board.
    - Gần neck -> warmer / bassier sound.
    - Gần bridge ->  brighter / treblier sound.
- **Độ rộng**: It is commonly believed (but disagreed by some) that:
    - If the pickup is thin (like single coils), it will produce a thinner but more articulate sound.
    - If the pickup is thick (like soap bars), it produce a thicker but less articulate sound.
    - Humbuckers are the pinnacle of thick pickups. They are very thick and full, but can get muddy very easily.
- **Chất liệu**:
    - Alnico magnets produce a traditional sound
    - Ceramic magnets produce a modern, hi-fi sound

| **Pickups**          | **Application** | **Emphasizes**   | **Good For**                                                          |
| -------------------- | --------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Neck humbucker       | Gibson EB-2     | Sub-bass         | Muddiness  <br>Deep bass  <br>Dub                                     |
| Neck + bridge single | Jazz Bass       | Bass + high mids | Articulation  <br>Soloing  <br>Tapping  <br>Slapping  <br>Ample bands |
| Mid humbucker        | Precision Bass  | Low mids         | Groove  <br>Support  <br>Loud or crowded bands                        |
| Bridge Humbucker     | StingRay        | Bass + treble    | Slapping  <br>Growl  <br>In-your-face aggression                      |
