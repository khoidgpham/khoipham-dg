Lou Rosenfeld, in his book Search Analytics for Your Site, talks about two areas of analysis within your internal search: • Pattern Analysis: What trends do you see in how people search your site? Are they using technical terms or more general terms? Are they depending on synonyms? Are they using jargon or abbreviations? • Failure Analysis: Are common search terms returning non-relevant or, even worse, no results?
