Brain activity and other physiologicalprocesses are regulated into patterns known as ==circadian rhythms==.

==melatonin== can help people cope with jet lag and shift work, both of which interfere with circadian rhythms (Petrie, Dawson, Thompson, & Brook, 1993)

==Insomnia== is a sleep disorder in which people’s mental health and ability to function are compromised by their inability to sleep

 One factor that complicates the estimation of how many people have insomnia is that many people who believe they are poor sleepers overestimate how long it takes them to fall asleep and often underestimate how much sleep they get on a typical night
